Test your Tor bridge

Are you wondering if your new Tor bridge works? You have come to the right place! This service lets you test any kind of bridge—be it vanilla, scramblesuit, obfs2, obfs3, or obfs4.

Enter your bridge’s bridge line, then click “Test”. This service will then try to bootstrap a Tor connection over your bridge, and tell you if it succeeded.

Examples of valid bridge lines are:

  • 1.2.3.4:443
  • 1.2.3.4:8080 1234567890ABCDEF1234567890ABCDEF12345678
